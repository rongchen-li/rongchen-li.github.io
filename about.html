Rongchen Li


Columbia Business School




I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in the Accounting Division at Columbia Business School, entering the 2024-2025 accounting job market.

My research focuses on the production, dissemination, and processing of information in capital markets, with an emphasis on how regulation and information intermediaries (re)shape information flows and economic agents' behaviors.

Before pursuing my PhD at Columbia, I earned my Master's degree in Accounting and Fundamental Analysis from Columbia University and Bachelor's degree in Accounting from Tsinghua University.
